ViXlate
Video Translation Using Artificial Intelligence
Overview

As part of our "Application of Deep Learning" course at Purdue University, we were tasked with developing a real-world project utilizing deep learning models. Our diverse team, composed of members from different regions, quickly recognized that language barriers could impede our collaboration. This insight drew our attention to a significant global issue: the language barriers that restrict access to educational content. To address this challenge, we decided to focus on developing an automated video translation system using deep learning, specifically aimed at educational content. Our goal was to create a solution that would not only facilitate seamless communication within our team but also help students and educators worldwide access and share knowledge more effectively. By leveraging advanced neural networks, we aimed to provide accurate, context-aware translations that maintain the original meaning and educational value of the videos.
